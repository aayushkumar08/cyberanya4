Cyberanya Activities

Software Showcase

Introduction: The Software Showcase at CyberAnya 4.0 invites participants to present their self-made software, applications, tools, or systems. This activity encourages innovation and real-world solution development.

Objectives:

  • Promote real-world software development skills.
  • Encourage creativity and product-based thinking.
  • Provide a platform to present projects to judges and peers.
  • Build confidence in showcasing tech talent.

Activity Rules:

  • Software must be partially or fully functional.
  • Teams/Individuals must provide a live demo or recorded demo.
  • Source code or documentation must be shown when requested.
  • Time limit: 8 minutes for demo + 2 minutes Q&A.

Judging Criteria:

  • Functionality & Innovation — 30%
  • Usefulness & Problem-Solving — 25%
  • UI/UX & Design — 20%
  • Presentation & Demo — 15%
  • Code Quality / Documentation — 10%

Recognitions & Rewards: Winners will receive certificates, medals, and project showcase spotlights. Outstanding software may also receive special recognition or potential mentorship opportunities.

PPT Presentation

Introduction: Welcome to the PPT Presentation session at CyberAnya 4.0. This session provides a platform for participants to showcase their research, innovative ideas, and projects through engaging PowerPoint presentations. This workshop is completely non-chargeable as the panel has decided a fee of 0 INR / person.

Objectives:

  • Encourage participants to present their ideas clearly and effectively.
  • Foster creativity and innovation in the field of cybersecurity.
  • Provide a platform for knowledge sharing and collaboration.

Activity Rules:

  • Each presentation should be no longer than 10 minutes.
  • Participants must use PowerPoint or equivalent presentation software.
  • All presentations must be submitted for review at least 48 hours before the session.
  • Participants should be prepared to answer questions from judges and the audience.

Judging Criteria:

  • Content Relevance and Quality — 30%
  • Clarity and Organization — 20%
  • Visual Appeal — 20%
  • Content Delivery and Engagement — 20%
  • Q&A Handling — 10%

Recognitions & Rewards: All participants will receive certificates of participation. Winners will be awarded trophies and recognized during the closing ceremony. Outstanding presentations may receive additional gift vouchers.

Poster Presentation

Introduction: The Poster Presentation at CyberAnya 4.0 allows participants to creatively communicate ideas, concepts, research, or awareness topics through an informative poster.

Objectives:

  • Enhance visual communication skills.
  • Encourage creativity and message clarity.
  • Promote research and idea-based expression.

Activity Rules:

  • Poster size: A3 or chart paper.
  • Handmade or digital posters are allowed.
  • Participants must explain their poster in 3 minutes.
  • No harmful or sensitive content is permitted.

Judging Criteria:

  • Creativity & Visual Appeal — 30%
  • Clarity of Message — 25%
  • Research Depth & Accuracy — 20%
  • Design Balance & Readability — 15%
  • Presentation — 10%

Recognitions & Rewards: Participants receive certificates. Top posters receive trophies, event display features, and special recognition.

Gaming Arena — BGMI & Free Fire

Introduction: The Gaming Arena at CyberAnya 4.0 features competitive tournaments in BGMI and Free Fire, offering an exciting esports experience for participants.

Objectives:

  • Promote esports talent and team coordination.
  • Encourage strategic gameplay and decision making.
  • Provide an energetic and competitive gaming environment.

Activity Rules:

  • Participants must join the match lobby on time.
  • Any form of cheating, hacking, or unfair play leads to disqualification.
  • Solo / Duo / Squad formats may vary based on entries.
  • Device overheating or network issues are the player's responsibility.

Judging / Win Criteria:

  • Match Rank — 50%
  • Kill Count — 30%
  • Survival Time — 10%
  • Team Coordination / Fair Play — 10%

Recognitions & Rewards: Winners receive trophies, medals, certificates, and special shoutouts. Top players may also be featured on the event page.
